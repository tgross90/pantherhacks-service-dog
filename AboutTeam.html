ABOUT THE TEAM

This website was started up by participants of FIU PantherHacks 2020. The theme was COVID 19 and we thought this was a great solution to provide applicants and service dog organizations with the resources to function during the pandemic. The team either have a service dog or they're applying for one. Either way this was a cause the two of them are passionate about.

Marina Alonzo is an English major at FIU and currently does not have a service dog, however she is hoping to apply for one in the near future. This is one of the causes that is dear to her heart because she knows that dogs can do so much to soothe the mind, body, and soul. She does have two family dogs named Oreo and Buttercup who are six. They are her two favorite pups and will always be dear to her heart.
